SCHOOL MOTTO

Nurturing a Stable Foundation.

MISSION

Anchor Junior School strives to provide excellence in education, in a caring, friendly environment, where each pupil is nurtured and shaped by values, has the opportunity to achieve full potential and to develop a passion for life through learning.

OUR VISION

Nurture each student's personal aspirations within a vibrant learner-centered community.

Statement of Values

In partnership with parents, Anchor Junior School pledges to integrate these values, not only in our policies but also in our daily lives:

  • Respect for oneself, others, and the environment
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Compassion and kindness
  • Excellence and perseverance
  • Responsibility and accountability
  • Collaboration and community
